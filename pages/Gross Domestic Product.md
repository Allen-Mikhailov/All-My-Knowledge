alias:: [[GDP]]

- The value of all goods and services created by a country over a period of time
- Symbol: $Y$
- Intermediate goods are not counted
	- Ex: iron used to make pipes is not counted
- Resold or used goods are also excluded
- It is Closely related to the [[Gross National Product]]
- Does not account for
	- Household production
	- Underground economy
	- Leisure time
	- Pollution and its problems
	- Crime and social Problems
	- Inequality
- Two approaches to calculate
	- Income Approach: $Y=W+R+I+P$
	  wages, rent, interest, profit
	- Expenditures approach: $Y=C+I+G+NX$
	  Consumption, Investment, Government Purchases, Net exports - Net imports
